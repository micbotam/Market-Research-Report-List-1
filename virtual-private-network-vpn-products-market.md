<p><p>The VPN products market includes Router VPN, Switch VPN, and Firewall VPN, each serving distinct roles in network security. Router VPNs establish secure connections for multiple devices through routers, enabling encrypted internet access. Switch VPNs facilitate secure communication between devices on a local area network, ensuring data protection within the network. Firewall VPNs integrate security measures directly into firewalls, controlling and monitoring traffic while providing secure remote access. Together, these products enhance network security and privacy for organizations and individuals.</p></p>
