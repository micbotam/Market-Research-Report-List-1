<p><p>The PV switch disconnector market is segmented into single-phase and three-phase types. Single-phase disconnectors are typically used in residential solar installations, providing an efficient means to isolate the system for maintenance or safety. They handle lower voltage and current levels, making them suitable for small-scale applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
