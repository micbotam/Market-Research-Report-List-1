<p><p>The medical angioplasty balloon market includes several types, each serving specific purposes in vascular procedures. Normal balloons are used to dilate narrowed arteries. Drug-eluting balloons combine mechanical dilation with localized drug delivery to reduce restenosis. Cutting balloons feature micro-incisions to facilitate plaque treatment effectively. Scoring balloons have spiral blades that create grooves in the plaque, enhancing vessel expansion. Together, these balloon types cater to diverse patient needs and improve the outcomes of angioplasty procedures across various cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
