<p><p>The C-MET HGF inhibitors market includes various types aimed at targeting the C-MET signaling pathway. C-Met biologic inhibitors involve larger molecules like monoclonal antibodies that disrupt C-MET functions. Small molecule C-Met inhibitors are designed to penetrate cells and inhibit the C-MET protein directly. HGF antagonistic antibodies block hepatocyte growth factor, preventing its interaction with C-MET. C-Met antagonistic antibodies, such as MetMAb, specifically target the C-MET receptor. HGF kringle variant antagonists disrupt HGF activity, providing additional therapeutic avenues.</p></p>
