<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Bonding</li><li>Room Temperature Bonding</li><li>High Temperature Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphite binders market comprises three main types based on bonding temperatures. Low temperature bonding occurs at temperatures below 100°C, often used for sensitive applications requiring minimal thermal impact. Room temperature bonding involves the application of binders under ambient conditions, offering convenience and versatility in various industrial processes. High temperature bonding, exceeding 300°C, ensures robust adhesion for applications subjected to extreme heat, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive sectors. Each type caters to specific performance and thermal requirements.</p></p>
