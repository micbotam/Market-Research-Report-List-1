<p><strong>The Aircraft Ice Protection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic De-icing Boots</li><li>Electro-thermal</li><li>Electro-mechanical</li><li>TKS Ice Protection</li><li>Bleed air</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Ice Protection Systems market includes various technologies to prevent or remove ice accumulation on aircraft surfaces. Pneumatic de-icing boots use inflatable rubber surfaces to break off ice. Electro-thermal systems utilize electrical heating elements to melt ice. Electro-mechanical systems employ mechanical means for ice removal. TKS ice protection relies on fluid-filled membranes that create a protective barrier. Bleed air systems utilize hot air from engines to prevent ice formation. Passive systems rely on aerodynamic designs to minimize ice buildup.</p></p>
