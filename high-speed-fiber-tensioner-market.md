<p><ul><li>"Single Mode Fiber"</li><li>"Polarization Maintaining Fiber"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed fiber tensioner market is primarily divided into two types: single mode fiber and polarization maintaining fiber. Single mode fiber is designed for long-distance communication, offering low attenuation and higher bandwidth capabilities. It transmits only one mode of light, enhancing signal quality. In contrast, polarization maintaining fiber maintains the polarization state of light, which is essential for certain applications in telecommunications and sensing. Both types require precise tension management in their installation and operation to ensure optimal performance and reliability.</p></p>
