<p><p>The medical gas mixer market comprises various types, including Air, Oxygen, Nitric Oxide (NO), and Nitrous Oxide. Air mixers blend atmospheric air with other gases for respiratory support. Oxygen mixers are crucial in delivering precise oxygen concentrations for patients with respiratory conditions. Nitric Oxide is utilized for its vasodilatory properties, particularly in treating pulmonary hypertension. Nitrous Oxide serves as an anesthetic agent, providing sedation and analgesia during procedures. Each type plays a vital role in modern healthcare for patient management.</p></p>
