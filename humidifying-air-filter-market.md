<p><p>The humidifying air filter market consists of two primary types: pure type and humidification type. Pure type filters focus on improving air quality by removing pollutants and allergens without adding moisture, making them ideal for drier environments. In contrast, humidification type filters not only cleanse the air but also introduce humidity, enhancing indoor air quality and comfort. This type is particularly beneficial in areas prone to dryness, helping to maintain optimal humidity levels while filtering out contaminants.</p></p>
