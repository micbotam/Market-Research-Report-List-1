<p><ul><li>Metallic Deoxidizer</li><li>Non-metallic Deoxidizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food Deoxidizer market is categorized into metallic and non-metallic types. Metallic deoxidizers typically include agents like iron powder, which actively absorb oxygen, thereby preserving food freshness and extending shelf life. Non-metallic deoxidizers, on the other hand, utilize substances such as silica gel or activated carbon to shield food from oxygen exposure, preventing spoilage. Both types are crucial in packaging and storage solutions, ensuring optimal food quality and safety while catering to different consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
