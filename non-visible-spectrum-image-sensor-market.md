<p><p>The non-visible spectrum image sensor market is primarily segmented into linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images by scanning a scene line by line, making them ideal for applications like barcode scanning and high-speed photography. Area image sensors, on the other hand, capture images in a two-dimensional grid, enabling instant image capture over a broader field of view, commonly used in digital cameras and medical imaging. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries.</p></p>
