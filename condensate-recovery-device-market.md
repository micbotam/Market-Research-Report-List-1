<p><ul><li>Open</li><li>Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The condensate recovery device market is categorized into open and closed systems. Open market systems allow condensate to be released into the atmosphere, relying on ambient conditions for recovery, often suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, closed market systems capture and reuse condensate within a sealed environment, enhancing efficiency and minimizing waste. Closed systems are typically more effective for larger operations, as they ensure greater control over condensate flow and quality, promoting sustainability and operational cost savings.</p></p>
