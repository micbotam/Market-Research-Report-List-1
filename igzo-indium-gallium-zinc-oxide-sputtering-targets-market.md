<p><p>The IGZO sputtering targets market is segmented into two primary types: planar targets and rotary targets. Planar targets are flat, offering uniform film deposition over a surface, making them ideal for applications in display technology. In contrast, rotary targets are cylindrical, providing a longer service life and higher deposition rates due to their continuous rotation during sputtering. Both target types are crucial for producing high-performance thin films in various electronic applications, particularly in OLED and LCD manufacturing.</p></p>
