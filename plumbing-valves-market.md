<p><p>The plumbing valves market encompasses various types founded on their design and functionality. Gate valves are used for on/off control, providing minimal flow resistance. Globe valves regulate flow and pressure effectively. Stop valves, often called stopcocks, control water flow in plumbing systems. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring unidirectional flow. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities with a spherical disc. Lastly, butterfly valves are lightweight and suitable for large volumes, utilizing a rotating disc to control flow. Each type serves distinct operational needs in plumbing systems.</p></p>
