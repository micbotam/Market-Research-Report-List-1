<p><p>The micro pumps market is categorized into several types, including diaphragm, piston, gear, and others. Diaphragm micropumps utilize a flexible diaphragm to create flow, ideal for precise dosing applications. Piston micropumps employ a moving piston to generate pressure and flow, suitable for high-thrust operations. Gear micropumps use rotating gears to move fluids efficiently, often in industrial settings. Other types encompass various innovative designs and technologies aimed at specialized applications, expanding the market's versatility and functionality.</p></p>
