<p><p>The hand-like robot gripper market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and servo-electric. Pneumatic grippers utilize compressed air for actuation, offering lightweight and fast operation, ideal for handling soft materials. Hydraulic grippers leverage fluid pressure, providing powerful gripping force suitable for heavy or dense objects. Servo-electric grippers employ electric motors for precise and programmable control, allowing for versatile and adaptive handling of various items. Each type serves distinct applications based on their operational characteristics and requirements.</p></p>
