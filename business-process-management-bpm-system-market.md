<p><p>Business Process Management (BPM) systems are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based BPM solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. They enable organizations to quickly implement and update processes. On-premises BPM systems, by contrast, are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization but usually requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves distinct business needs and preferences.</p></p>
