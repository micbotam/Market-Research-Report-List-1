<p><p>The Night Vision Enhancement Systems market comprises two primary types: Near-infrared (NIR) and Far-infrared (FIR). NIR technology utilizes wavelengths just beyond the visible spectrum, enhancing images in low-light conditions while relying on ambient light sources. FIR, on the other hand, detects thermal radiation emitted by objects, producing clear images even in complete darkness by capturing heat signatures. Each type serves distinct applications, including military, security, and surveillance, catering to specific operational needs in nighttime environments.</p></p>
