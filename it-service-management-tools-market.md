<p><p>IT Service Management (ITSM) tools are categorized into two primary market types: On-Premise and Cloud. On-Premise ITSM tools are installed locally on an organization's infrastructure, offering complete control and customization. However, they require significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-based ITSM tools are hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, lower initial costs, and easier updates. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific needs, budget, and IT strategy, balancing control versus flexibility.</p></p>
