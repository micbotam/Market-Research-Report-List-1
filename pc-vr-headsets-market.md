<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PC VR Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3DOF Motion Tracking Type</li><li>6DOF Motion Tracking Type</li><li>9DOF Motion Tracking Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PC VR headsets are categorized into three main types based on motion tracking capabilities. The 3DOF (Degrees of Freedom) type tracks head rotation but not position, suitable for stationary experiences. The 6DOF type adds positional tracking, allowing users to move freely within a defined space, enhancing immersion. The 9DOF type combines both gyroscopic and accelerometer data for highly accurate motion tracking, enabling complex interactions in virtual environments. Each type caters to different user needs and experiences in VR applications.</p></p>
