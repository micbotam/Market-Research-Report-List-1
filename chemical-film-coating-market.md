<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Topcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical film coating market is primarily divided into two main types: primer and topcoat. Primers are used as a preparatory layer to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance on metal surfaces, ensuring better performance of subsequent coatings. In contrast, topcoats serve as the final protective layer that provides durability, aesthetic appeal, and additional resistance against environmental factors. Both types are crucial in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications, to enhance the lifespan and functionality of coated materials.</p></p>
