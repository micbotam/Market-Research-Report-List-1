<p><p>The natural plant hair colorants market is primarily divided into two categories: permanent dyes and semi-permanent dyes. Permanent dyes provide long-lasting color by penetrating the hair shaft, offering a durable solution for greys and color changes. They typically require a developer and maintain color intensity until new growth appears. Conversely, semi-permanent dyes coat the hair without penetrating deeply, fading gradually over time with washes. They offer a temporary option for those seeking color without commitment or damage to the hair structure.</p></p>
