<p><ul><li>Side-Mount Floor Locks</li><li>Top-Mount Floor Locks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor locks are essential hardware for securing doors and gates in various environments. Side-mount floor locks are installed on the side of the door frame, providing an unobtrusive and space-saving solution, making them suitable for narrow openings. In contrast, top-mount floor locks are affixed to the top of the door, offering enhanced security for heavier doors and requiring more clearance. Both types serve to prevent door movement, ensuring safety and stability in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
