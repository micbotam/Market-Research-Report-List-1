<p><strong>The Voltage Regulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap-Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage regulators market comprises several types, including ferroresonant and tap-switching regulators. Ferroresonant regulators utilize magnetic components to maintain stable output voltage despite fluctuations in input voltage and load conditions, offering effective voltage stabilization and surge protection. Tap-switching regulators, on the other hand, adjust output voltage by switching between various transformer taps, providing flexibility for different voltage requirements. Both types are essential in various industries, ensuring reliable power supply and protection for sensitive electrical equipment.</p></p>
