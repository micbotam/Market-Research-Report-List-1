<p><p>The prepacked chromatography columns market comprises several key types, each serving distinct analytical and purification needs. Ion exchange chromatography separates molecules based on charge, making it ideal for protein purification. Affinity chromatography utilizes specific interactions for targeted separation, particularly useful in biopharmaceuticals. Multimodal chromatography combines multiple interactions for enhanced selectivity and efficiency. Gel filtration chromatography, or size exclusion chromatography, separates molecules based on size, suitable for purifying proteins and nucleic acids. Together, these techniques cater to diverse applications in research and industry.</p></p>
