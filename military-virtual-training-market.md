<p><p>The military virtual training market comprises two primary types: traditional military virtual training and virtual reality (VR) based military training. Traditional military virtual training utilizes computer-based simulations and software to replicate battlefield scenarios, allowing for realistic, low-cost training environments. In contrast, VR-based military training immerses personnel in fully interactive, 3D environments through advanced headsets and motion tracking, enhancing realism and engagement. Both types are essential for developing tactical skills and operational readiness while minimizing risks associated with live training exercises.</p></p>
