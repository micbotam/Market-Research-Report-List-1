<p><strong>The Aircraft Navigation Aids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-directional Beacons</li><li>Instrument Landing Systems (ILS)</li><li>Distance Measuring Equipment (DME)</li><li>Doppler VHF Omnidirectional Range (DVOR)</li><li>Inertial Navigation System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Navigation Aids market comprises various types essential for safe and precise navigation. Non-directional Beacons (NDB) provide radio signals for pilots to determine their location. Instrument Landing Systems (ILS) aid in landing by guiding aircraft during low visibility. Distance Measuring Equipment (DME) calculates the distance to a beacon. Doppler VHF Omnidirectional Range (DVOR) enhances course accuracy using Doppler effects. Inertial Navigation Systems utilize internal sensors for self-contained navigation. Other aids include satellite-based systems and ground-based technologies, enhancing overall air travel safety.</p></p>
