<p><strong>The IoT RFID Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Sensors</li><li>High Frequency Sensors</li><li>UHF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID sensor market comprises three main types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF) sensors operate at 125-134 kHz, offering short read ranges and better performance in challenging environments, making them ideal for animal tracking and access control. High Frequency (HF) sensors, operating at 13.56 MHz, provide moderate range and data transfer rates, commonly used in smart labels and payment systems. Ultra High Frequency (UHF) sensors, ranging from 860-960 MHz, support long-range reading and high-speed data transfer, suitable for inventory management and logistics applications.</p></p>
