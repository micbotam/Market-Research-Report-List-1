<p><strong>The Hot Water Buffer Tank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Buffer Tank</li><li>Air Bag Buffer Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot water buffer tank market encompasses two main types: diaphragm buffer tanks and air bag buffer tanks. Diaphragm buffer tanks utilize a flexible diaphragm to separate water and air, maintaining pressure stability and enhancing energy efficiency. In contrast, air bag buffer tanks feature an internal air-filled bag that expands and contracts with water volume changes, providing similar pressure management benefits. Both tank types are essential for optimizing hot water systems, improving thermal storage, and ensuring consistent hot water supply in various applications.</p></p>
