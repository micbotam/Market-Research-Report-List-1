<p><p>The Dual Power Transfer Switches market is segmented into two main types: automatic and manual switches. Automatic transfer switches (ATS) facilitate seamless transition between primary and backup power sources without human intervention, ensuring reliability during power outages. In contrast, manual transfer switches require operators to manually switch power sources, offering control but less convenience. Both types serve crucial roles in various applications, including residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, allowing facilities to maintain continuous power supply during interruptions.</p></p>
