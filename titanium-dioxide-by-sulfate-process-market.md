<p><p>The Titanium Dioxide market produced via the sulfate process primarily consists of two types: Rutile and Anatase. Rutile Titanium Dioxide, known for its high refractive index and durability, is widely used in coatings, plastics, and paper industries. It offers excellent opacity and color stability. Conversely, Anatase Titanium Dioxide is less expensive and primarily used in applications requiring lower performance, such as photocatalysis and some types of plastics. Both types serve distinct market needs within various industrial applications.</p></p>
