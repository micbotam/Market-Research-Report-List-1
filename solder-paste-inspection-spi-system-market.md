<p><p>Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) systems are essential in the electronics manufacturing process to ensure the quality of solder paste application. In-line SPI systems are integrated into the production line, allowing real-time inspection of solder paste deposits on PCBs as they are processed. This enables immediate feedback and corrective actions. Off-line SPI systems, on the other hand, operate separately from the production line, allowing for extensive analysis and inspection of solder paste batches after application, thus providing detailed insights into quality control.</p></p>
