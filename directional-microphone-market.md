<p><strong>The Directional Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Unidirectional Type"</li><li>"Bidirectional Type"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The directional microphone market consists of two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional. Unidirectional microphones capture sound primarily from one direction, making them ideal for isolating the intended audio source while minimizing background noise. This type is commonly used in interviews and performances. In contrast, bidirectional microphones pick up sound from two opposite directions, which is useful for capturing dialogue between two speakers or creating unique audio effects. Both types are essential in various applications, including broadcasting, recording, and sound reinforcement.</p></p>
