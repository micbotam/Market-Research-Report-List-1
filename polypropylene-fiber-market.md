<p><p>The polypropylene fiber market is categorized primarily into three types: long fiber, staple fiber, and others. Long fibers are continuous strands used for applications like carpets and geotextiles, offering strength and durability. Staple fibers, on the other hand, consist of shorter lengths, making them ideal for textiles, upholstery, and non-woven products due to their flexibility and ease of processing. The "others" category includes specialty fibers and blends used in various niche applications, expanding the versatility of polypropylene fibers in diverse industries.</p></p>
