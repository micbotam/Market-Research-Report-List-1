<p><p>The metal roof decking market primarily consists of two types: A Roof Deck and B Roof Deck. A Roof Deck features a flat, structural profile designed to support various roofing systems while providing superior load-bearing capacity and stability. It is often used in commercial and industrial buildings. B Roof Deck, on the other hand, has a deeper profile, offering enhanced insulation and acoustic performance, making it suitable for applications requiring sound attenuation and thermal efficiency. Both types cater to specific design and functionality needs in construction.</p></p>
