<p><p>Surfboard wax is essential for enhancing traction on surfboards, and it typically comes in two primary types: base coat wax and top coat wax. Base coat wax is applied first to create a foundational layer that ensures better grip and adherence of the top coat. The top coat wax, applied over the base, offers additional traction and is often designed for specific water temperatures. Together, they optimize performance and stability while surfing, catering to varying conditions and individual preferences.</p></p>
