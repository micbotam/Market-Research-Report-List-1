<p><p>Worm gear drives are specialized mechanical devices used to transfer power and motion between non-parallel shafts. The market is divided into two main types: left-hand and right-hand worm gear drives. Left-hand worm gear drives feature a left-hand thread, allowing for specific applications where counterclockwise rotation is needed. Conversely, right-hand worm gear drives utilize a right-hand thread for clockwise motion. Each type serves different mechanical and industrial requirements, being chosen based on the desired rotational direction and application demands.</p></p>
