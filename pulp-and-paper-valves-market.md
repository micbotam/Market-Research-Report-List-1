<p><p>The pulp and paper valves market comprises three main types: electric valves, pneumatic valves, and manual valves. Electric valves rely on electrical signals for automation, providing precise control and efficiency in processing. Pneumatic valves use compressed air to operate, ensuring rapid actuation and robust performance in challenging environments. Manual valves require physical adjustment by operators, offering simplicity and reliability for straightforward applications. Together, these valves play a crucial role in managing fluid flow and maintaining system integrity in pulp and paper production.</p></p>
