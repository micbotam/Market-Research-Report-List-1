<p><strong>The Fuel Retail Terminal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Systems</li><li>Suction Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fuel Retail Terminal market comprises two primary types: submersible systems and suction systems. Submersible systems involve pumps located below the fuel storage tank, providing efficient and quiet operation while minimizing vapor emissions. In contrast, suction systems use above-ground pumps that draw fuel from the storage tanks, often allowing for easier maintenance but potentially leading to higher operational costs. Both systems cater to diverse retail needs, influencing factors like installation costs, operational efficiency, and environmental compliance.</p></p>
