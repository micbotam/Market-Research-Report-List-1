<p><ul><li>MEMS Accelerometer</li><li>MEMS Gyroscope</li><li>MEMS IMU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive MEMS Inertial Sensor market includes three main types: MEMS Accelerometers, MEMS Gyroscopes, and MEMS Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs). MEMS Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, enabling vehicle stability and control systems. MEMS Gyroscopes assess angular velocity, crucial for navigation and electronic stability systems. MEMS IMUs combine both accelerometers and gyroscopes, providing comprehensive motion sensing for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous vehicles. Together, these sensors enhance safety, maneuverability, and overall driving experience in modern automobiles.</p></p>
