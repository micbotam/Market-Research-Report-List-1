<p><p>Coining presses are categorized based on their molding capacity, measured in kilonewtons (kN). The market is segmented into four types: </p><p>1. **Under 1000 kN**: Ideal for small-scale production, suitable for precision applications in delicate materials.  </p><p>2. **1000-2000 kN**: Common in moderate production, balancing power and versatility for medium-sized components.  </p><p>3. **2000-3000 kN**: Used for heavy-duty applications, often in automotive and industrial sectors, handling robust materials.  </p><p>4. **Above 3000 kN**: Designed for high-performance, large-scale manufacturing, capable of processing thick metals and complex shapes.</p></p>
