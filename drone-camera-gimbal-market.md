<p><p>The drone camera gimbal market is categorized into various types based on stabilization axes: 2-axis, 3-axis, 4-axis, and others. A 2-axis gimbal stabilizes pitch and roll, reducing vibrations for smoother footage. A 3-axis gimbal includes an additional yaw axis, providing enhanced stability and versatility for aerial filming. A 4-axis gimbal extends this stabilization further, compensating for additional movements, often found in advanced models. Other types may include specialized or multi-axis systems designed for unique applications, such as film production or surveying.</p></p>
