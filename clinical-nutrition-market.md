<p><p>The Clinical Nutrition market encompasses three main types: Oral Nutrition, Enteral Nutrition, and Parenteral Nutrition. Oral Nutrition products are designed for patients who can consume food orally but may require supplementation for better health. Enteral Nutrition includes formulas delivered directly into the gastrointestinal tract for those who cannot eat normally, while Parenteral Nutrition involves nutrient delivery directly into the bloodstream for patients with severe digestive issues. Each type addresses specific nutritional needs for improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
